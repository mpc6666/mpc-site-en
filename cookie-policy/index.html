Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and provide information to site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Micro-Precision Carbide uses cookies only for essential functions and to remember your privacy preferences. We do not use cookies for marketing, advertising, or third-party tracking.

3. Currently Active Cookies

Based on current implementation, this website sets only one essential cookie:

4. Third-Party Services and Functionality

This website uses GetForm (getform.io) to process form submissions (e.g., contact forms). This service is essential for website functionality but does not set cookies on your browser for basic form operations. Form data is processed directly by GetForm’s servers.

Automated scanning tools may detect this integration as a “necessary” functionality, but no actual cookies are set unless explicitly required for enhanced features (which are not currently implemented).

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage your consent at any time:

  • Through our banner: Use the accept/reject buttons when you first visit.
  • Through your browser: Configure your browser settings to refuse cookies. Please note that blocking cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

For any questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us at info@mpccarbide.com.
